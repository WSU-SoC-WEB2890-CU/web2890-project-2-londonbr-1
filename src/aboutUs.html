About Us

During the COVID-19 pandemic, Stacy Harfert and her sons began crafting unique ice cream bars to sell at local events, noticing a gap in the market for vibrant flavors and designs. Drawing from her background in product development, Stacy infused her all-natural vanilla ice cream with colorful chocolate drizzles, creating the distinct Roonies ice cream bars.

The overwhelming positive response led to the establishment of Roonies Ice Cream, equipped with a commercial kitchen producing over a million bars annually. Their products, celebrated for their taste and festive appearance, are now available in numerous convenience stores, event centers, and recreational outlets across Utah.

In just three years, Roonies continues to engage with local communities through events and their philanthropic efforts cementing their presence as a beloved hometown brand.
