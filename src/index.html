Products

Roonies Ice Cream offers ice cream bars in five flavors. We start with a local dairy who provides our own ice cream recipe, then churn into delicious ice cream, hand dip the ice cream bars in a high quality chocolate, and finish with a festive chocolate drizzle.

Ice Cream Bars

Each ice cream sandwich is meticulously crafted, starting with our high-quality 14% butterfat ice cream, churned slowly to achieve the perfect creamy texture. The ice cream is then paired with a scrumptious cookie, creating a harmonious combination of flavors. For an added touch of decadence, we finish our ice cream sandwiches with a delicate chocolate drizzle or a roll of sprinkles.

Ice Cream Sandwhiches

Packaged conveniently in 5oz cups with a spoon in the lid, choose from the rich and satisfying Java Chip, cool and refreshing Mint Chip, the exotic and creamy Horchata, the timeless Cookies 'n Cream, the delectable Cookie Dough, or the indulgent Salted Caramel, this collection of Roonies ice cream flavors offers a delightful array of options to please every palate.

Our Story

During the COVID-19 pandemic, Stacy Harfert and her sons began crafting unique ice cream bars to sell at local events, noticing a gap in the market for vibrant flavors and designs. Drawing from her background in product development, Stacy infused her all-natural vanilla ice cream with colorful chocolate drizzles, creating the distinct Roonies ice cream bars.
